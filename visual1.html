Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator(0 Degrees Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time ofthe year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
