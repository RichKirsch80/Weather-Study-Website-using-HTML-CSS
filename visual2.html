Humidity

As you can see in the graph, humidity appears not to be affected by Latitude. It is noteable that at the extreme latitudes there is a gap in the data. This is likely due to the lack of city data for these areas as you get closer to the North and South Pole. A solution would be seeking out research stations or citizens of these latitudes to provide humidity levels to address this short sight.
